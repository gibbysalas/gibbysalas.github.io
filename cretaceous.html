The Cretaceous Period

The final act of the Mesozoic Era, the Cretaceous was a time of explosive evolution and dramatic endings. Flowering plants emerged, reshaping ecosystems and attracting new insect life. Dinosaurs reached their peak in size, diversity, and ferocity.

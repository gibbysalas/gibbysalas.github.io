The Cretaceous Period

The final era of the dinosaurs was also their most dramatic. The continents drifted apart, climates shifted, and ecosystems diversified. Dinosaurs grew larger, smarter, and more specialized—culminating in giants like Tyrannosaurus rex and clever hunters like Velociraptor.

🦖 Cretaceous Celebrities

  • Tyrannosaurus rex – The apex predator, with bone-crushing jaws.
  • Triceratops – A horned herbivore with armor-like frills.
  • Velociraptor – Small but strategic, with feathers and deadly claws.
  • Ankylosaurus – A walking tank, protected by bony plates and a club tail.
  • Parasaurolophus – Known for its crest that might’ve been used to sing!

🌸 Flora of the Cretaceous

Flowering plants bloomed across the world, attracting pollinators and changing the plant game forever. Forests also included conifers, ferns, and cycads, but angiosperms stole the spotlight.

☄️ The End of an Era

About 66 million years ago, a massive asteroid struck Earth, triggering climate chaos and mass extinction. While many species vanished, others—like birds—took flight into a new age.
